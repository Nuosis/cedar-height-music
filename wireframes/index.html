What age range do you teach? +
We welcome students of all ages, from young children (age 5+) to adults. Our teaching approach adapts to each student's developmental stage and learning style.
How long are the lessons? +
Standard lessons are 30 minutes for most students. We also offer 45-minute and 60-minute sessions for advanced students or those who prefer longer sessions.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Lessons cancelled with proper notice can be rescheduled within the same month.
